Garage door locked with no windows, but you're too weak to break it to access the juicy loot inside? Well, now with the right tools you can quietly open doors without the key, with some patience and luck!

description=- A craftable Forged Lockpicking Tool Set - Improves lockpicking success chance over paperclip and screwdriver. Can be crafted at any forge with two small metal pieces. Recipe unlocks at Lockpicking level 3 and Blacksmithing level 3.
